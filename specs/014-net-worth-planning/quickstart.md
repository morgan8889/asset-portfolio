# Quickstart: Net Worth & FIRE Planning

## Overview
This feature allows you to track your total net worth and plan for financial independence (FIRE).

## Usage

### Tracking Net Worth
1. Navigate to the **Planning** page.
2. View the **Net Worth History** chart at the top.
3. If you have debts (mortgage, student loans), click **"Manage Liabilities"**.
4. Add your liabilities to see your true Net Worth (Assets - Liabilities).

### Planning for FIRE
1. On the Planning page, scroll to **FIRE Projection**.
2. Enter your **Annual Expenses** (e.g., $60,000).
3. Set your **Safe Withdrawal Rate** (default 4%).
4. Enter your **Monthly Savings** amount.
5. The chart updates instantly to show your "Crossover Point" (when Net Worth > FIRE Number).

### Running Scenarios
1. Click **"New Scenario"**.
2. Choose a type (e.g., "Market Correction").
3. Set parameters (e.g., -20% drop).
4. See how your FIRE date shifts in the projection.

## Implementation Details

- **Inflation**: Projections are in "Real Dollars" (today's purchasing power).
- **Privacy**: All goal data and scenarios are stored locally.
- **Integration**: Asset values update automatically from your Holdings.
